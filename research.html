Papers

Science development is important nowadays, therefore I am writing papers on subjects such as computer science or cryptography.

  • (December 24, 2015) Czyzewski Variation, forgotten hypermodern chess opening

    The idea came as a result of finding old notes from 1883 of my great-grandfather, who has been analyzing two different positions with curious concept - "Delayed Fianchetto Opening". Practice has shown that it is a great position for playing Blitz and Bullets games.

  • (June 5, 2015) Naive Czyzewski Generator, a different approach

    This document describes the NCG pseudo-random function family that passes all the Dieharder and TestU01 tests, it also provides examples, comments and analysis.
